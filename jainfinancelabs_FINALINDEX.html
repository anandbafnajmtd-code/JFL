Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
What if the market crashes after I invest?+
In a balanced portfolio with SIP, you actually buy MORE units when market is low — rupee-cost averaging. Historical data shows markets always recover. During COVID 2020, those who stayed invested saw massive returns by 2021–24.
How much should I invest in mutual funds?+
It depends on your income, emergency fund, goals, and risk appetite. A common starting point is 20–30% of monthly savings. We always personalize — we don't give generic numbers without understanding your situation. Even ₹500/month started early beats ₹5,000/month started late.
Is LIC a good investment? Should I continue my policy?+
Traditional LIC endowment plans give 4–5% returns — barely beating inflation. LIC term insurance is excellent. Our recommendation: keep term insurance, evaluate endowment policies case-by-case. We'll honestly review your specific policy.
How do you earn money? Are you really unbiased?+
We earn commission from mutual fund houses and insurance companies — disclosed openly. We stay unbiased because our reputation depends on your long-term success. A client who trusts us long-term is worth far more than one bad recommendation.
Can I start a SIP with just ₹500/month?+
Yes! Many SIPs start from ₹500/month. Amount matters less than starting early. ₹500/month at 25 beats ₹5,000/month at 40 due to compounding power.
How is JFL different from Groww or Zerodha?+
Groww and Zerodha are platforms — they give you access but no real advice. We give you personalized guidance, hand-holding, and someone who actually picks up the phone. When markets crash, you won't be alone.
